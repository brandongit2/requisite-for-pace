a game by Brandon Tsang

Tetroid takes the classic game Tetris to a whole 'nother level. Experience the game you know and love, now in three dimensions. Now available on PC and Mac.

How to play

The game consists of blocks called tetrominoes. As they fall, your job is to maneuver them around to fit into the bottom of the grid. If you get an entire layer filled, that layer disappears and you gain points.

Use the QWEASD keys to rotate the tetromino.

Use the arrow keys to move the tetromino.
